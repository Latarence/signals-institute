Riviera 27

Events that keep the signal alive.

Riviera 27 is the Institute's public-facing rhythm: forums, workshops, and partner convenings that bring researchers, veterans, youth, and community stakeholders into the same room—repeatedly—until opportunity becomes normal.

Featured

Researcher Forum Series

Open Q&A with working researchers. All questions welcome: career, project, or pure curiosity. Hosted across libraries, community centers, and city facilities.
