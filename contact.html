Ways to connect

What we're looking for.

We partner with organizations and individuals who share our vision for expanding access to technical careers. Here's how you can get involved.

Host

Venues & Facilities

Libraries, schools, community centers, and city facilities that want to host technical programming.

Employ

Veteran Placement

Defense contractors and technical employers looking to hire veterans with security clearances.

Fund

Grants & Donations

Foundations, individuals, and organizations supporting workforce development in underserved communities.

Volunteer

Share your expertise.

Working researchers, engineers, and technical professionals can make a huge impact by sharing their experience. No formal teaching required—just authentic conversation about what you actually do.

Forum

Guest speaker

Present at a monthly researcher forum. Tell your story, answer questions, inspire the next generation.

Mentor

Youth mentorship

One-on-one guidance for students working on science fair projects or technical portfolios.

Career

Employer shadow

Site visits and career day participation. Show people what technical work actually looks like.
